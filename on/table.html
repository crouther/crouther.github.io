A new table for conversation

"Guiding the compass needle one step further. Without hesitation, I'm making the transitive, digital native culture wild. Bringing off the grid serinity to the cloud. Nature and technology at it's finest. This is the "Discussion Table"!"

Utilizing traditional pioneering diagonal lashing, the twine binds chainsaw cut pine wood logs together to craft the perfect outdoor workbench and mobile workstation desk. Elevated conveniently for work standing or sitting, it offers multi-position accessibility.
