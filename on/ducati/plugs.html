The Spark Plugs are a routine replacement depending on milage, time or general wear and tear. CHARTS.

Motorcycle Area References

  • Left side of Ducati Monster, Between Frame & Engine
  • Front bottom, behind oil cooler (if applicable) behind front fork

Materials

  • Spark plugs
    •
  • Anti-seize lubricant

Tools

  • Spark Plug Removal Tool (Ducati Specialized Part)
  • Steel bar tool

(Figure 2: Location of specialized Ducati spark plug removal tool)

1. Insert key near right rear exhaust to remove seat. If necessary, use a screw driver from the underside of the bike to remove fastening screws of the seat cowl before removing the seat. Unfasten the tank clip near the key ignition slot and lift the tank.

2. Locate spark plug cover cap. Lift cap off spark plug

3. Loosen plug with wrench, hand remove spark plug

4. Open box with new spark plug. Open anti-seize lubricant package. Apply anti-seie lubricant along the threading of the new spark plug.

5. Hand thread new spark plug into first spark plug engine hole. Insure snug placement by finishing the tightening with the specialize spark plug tool

6. Repeat steps for second spark plug.
