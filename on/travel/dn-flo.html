Florida: Everglades National Park, The Keys, Palm Beach, & Miami

Sometimes you need a place to... Relax. Retire. Retreat.

There's no mistake Florida is a winter getaway. What might feel like spring weather for some states is their coldest winter. A week in Palm Beach, Miami, and the Key's is enough to make someone retire early. The Everglades is dangerously intriguing with enough wonder to wet anyone's appetite.

There's a fine line between fishing and just standing on the shore like an idiot. The flight there I walked out of the lounge with the song, knock, knock, knockin' on heaven's door. And I arrived home with the song, all you need is love, love, love, love is all you need. That could easily describe each beach, bed and breakfast, and suburban 30's somthin' burro Florida hides. To the early couples, the University students beginning their careers, and the retires hiding in Florida's paradise.
