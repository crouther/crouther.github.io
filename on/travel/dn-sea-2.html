Tokyo, Japan. What started as two weeks of instant gratification quickly devolved into a month of wholesome living and humble beginnings. I planted roots in Fuji Kawaguchi-cho. Worked a house that became my home, to which I shared with only the best strangers from around the world. Marketing the foothills of Yamanashi and locale. When my time came, I was whisked away by an international dance group into Yokohama and back into Tokyo.
