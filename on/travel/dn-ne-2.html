(Statue of Liberty)

Scrambling for french toast in the morning with a busy baristas. Wandering the late night streets of New York to avoid noisey neighbors. Hustling to the next train to make the on time departure. Bumping into the girl next to you on the bus. Shouting from the roof tops I'm a creature of habit. Looking for the tickers of the New York Stock Exchange. Questioning the charging bull statue and it's popularity. Not knowing who's famous and who just want attention. This is the concret jungle. Where the girl at the bar will try and convince you can make it in the big city.

-July 11th, 2019
