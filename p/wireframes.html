Wireframes

The exoskeleton of the page. A bare bones drafting methodology to help visualize the placement of elements on a web page. Content such as images, text, and navigation are loosely described and drawn accordingly.

These low fidelity wireframes are used to transform lines, sketches and drawings into high fidelity custom templates and mockups.
